<p><p>Industrial Grade Hydrogel is available in two main types: Natural and Synthetic. </p><p>Natural hydrogels are derived from natural sources such as seaweed, animal tissues, or microbial fermentation. These hydrogels are biocompatible and biodegradable, making them suitable for various medical and industrial applications.</p><p>Synthetic hydrogels, on the other hand, are manufactured using chemical processes. They offer greater control over properties such as porosity, mechanical strength, and degradation rate. These hydrogels are widely used in areas such as drug delivery, tissue engineering, and wound healing.</p></p>
