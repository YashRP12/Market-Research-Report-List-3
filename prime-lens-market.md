Wide-angle lenses provide a broader field of view, making them ideal for landscape and architectural photography. Telephoto lenses have a longer focal length, allowing photographers to capture distant subjects with great detail, making them suitable for wildlife and sports photography. Each type of prime lens has its unique characteristics and applications in the photography market.</p></p>
