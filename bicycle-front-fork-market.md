<p><strong>The Bicycle Front Fork Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coil</li><li>Resistance Glue</li><li>Oil-Coil</li><li>Oil-Air</li><li>Air-Air</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bicycle front fork market offers a variety of types to choose from. Coil forks utilize a metal coil spring for suspension. Resistance glue forks use a viscous fluid to dampen shocks. Oil-coil forks combine coil springs with hydraulic oil for smoother suspension. Oil-air forks also use oil for damping but incorporate an air chamber for adjustable stiffness. Air-air forks feature air chambers for both suspension and damping, providing a lightweight and customizable option for riders.</p></p>
