<p><p>Electric sliding doors come in two main types: Top-mounted Type and Ground Rail Type. Top-mounted Type doors are installed with the supporting track mounted at the top of the door frame, allowing for smooth and quiet operation. Ground Rail Type doors have the track installed on the ground, providing stability and durability for heavy-duty use. Both types offer a convenient and automated solution for entryways in various commercial and residential settings, enhancing accessibility and convenience for users.</p></p>
