Plasticizers are used to improve the flexibility and durability of plastic components in vehicles. Anti-scratch agents help protect the exterior surfaces from damage. Stabilizers prevent degradation of materials due to heat and UV exposure. Antioxidants inhibit oxidation in materials, extending their lifespan.
