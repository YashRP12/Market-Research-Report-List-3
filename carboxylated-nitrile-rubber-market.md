<p><p>Carboxylated nitrile rubber is primarily classified into three types based on the percentage of acrylonitrile content: 8-18%, 18-22%, and 23-27% and above. The properties and applications of each type vary depending on the acrylonitrile content. The 8-18% acrylonitrile content type offers good flexibility and low temperature resistance. The 18-22% acrylonitrile content type provides a balance of chemical resistance and mechanical properties. The 23-27% and above acrylonitrile content type offers excellent oil and fuel resistance.</p></p>
