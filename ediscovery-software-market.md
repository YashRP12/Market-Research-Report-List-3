<p><p>The eDiscovery software market encompasses two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based eDiscovery software operates on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage legal data through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premise eDiscovery software is installed and operated on the organization's own servers, providing greater control and security over sensitive data. While it requires upfront infrastructure investment, it allows organizations to maintain full control over their legal data within their own premises.</p></p>
