The market offers several types, including Radial Type, Diode Type, Wire Bonding Type, Film Type, SMD Type, Wire Type, and Others. Radial Type sensors are cylindrical and commonly used in household appliances. Diode Type sensors have a built-in diode for temperature sensing and are suitable for precision applications. Wire Bonding Type sensors are small and reliable, often used in automotive and industrial sectors. Film Type sensors are thin and flexible, commonly used in medical devices. SMD Type sensors are surface mount devices with high accuracy. Wire Type sensors have a wire attached for easy installation. The Others category includes less common variations.</p></p>
