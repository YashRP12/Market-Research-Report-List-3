<p><strong>The Super-Finishing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Super-Finishing Machines are categorised into three main types based on their functioning and application. In-Line Super-Finishing Machines are designed for continuous processing of workpieces without manual intervention. CNC Super-Finishing Machines use computer numerical control technology for precision and automated operation. Drag Super-Finishing Machines are used for finishing a wide variety of workpieces by dragging an abrasive medium across the surface. Each type of machine offers specific advantages in terms of efficiency, accuracy, and versatility in the super-finishing process.</p></p>
