<p><p>The Telephone Answering Machine market is divided into two main types: Corded Answering Machines and Cordless Answering Machines. Corded Answering Machines are connected directly to a telephone line and are typically used in homes or small offices. Cordless Answering Machines, on the other hand, offer more flexibility as they are not bound by a physical connection to the telephone line. They are popular in larger offices and households where mobility is important. Both types of answering machines serve the purpose of recording and storing incoming messages when the user is unable to answer the phone.</p></p>
