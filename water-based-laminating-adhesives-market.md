This growth can be attributed to the rising awareness regarding the harmful effects of solvent-based adhesives on the environment and human health. Water-based laminating adhesives offer several advantages, including low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions, low odor, and ease of application.</p><p>Furthermore, the demand for water-based laminating adhesives has been driven by the growing food packaging industry, particularly in developing economies.
