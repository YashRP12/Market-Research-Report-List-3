<p><ul><li>Online Detection</li><li>Offline Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Partial discharge detection systems can be classified into two main types: online detection and offline detection. Online detection systems continuously monitor equipment for signs of partial discharge during normal operation, providing real-time alerts to potential issues. Offline detection systems, on the other hand, require the equipment to be taken offline for testing and analysis. Although online detection systems offer immediate feedback, offline detection systems may offer more comprehensive and detailed information during maintenance or inspection processes.</p></p>
