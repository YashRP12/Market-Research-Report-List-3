<p><p>Portable dry herb vaporizers can be categorized into two main types: Conduction and Convection. </p><p>Conduction type vaporizers heat the herb directly through contact with a heated surface, resulting in faster heating times and more consistent vapor production. On the other hand, Convection type vaporizers heat the herb by passing hot air through it, providing a more even and efficient heating process which can result in better flavor and vapor quality. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different preferences within the market.</p></p>
