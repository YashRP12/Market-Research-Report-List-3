<p><p>Commercial Printing AGVs are categorized into four main types based on their load-bearing capacity and functionality. Unit Load Type AGVs are designed to transport heavy loads on pallets or containers. Automated Forklift Type AGVs mimic the functions of traditional forklifts, enabling them to lift and stack loads independently. Tugger Type AGVs are used to tow multiple carts or trailers around a facility. Lastly, Other types of AGVs include custom-designed vehicles that cater to specific material handling needs in the commercial printing industry.</p></p>
