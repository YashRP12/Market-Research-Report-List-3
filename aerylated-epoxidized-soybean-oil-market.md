<p><p>Aerylated Epoxidized Soybean Oil is produced through two main methods: Ring Opening Polymerization and Reaction with Maleic Anhydride. In Ring Opening Polymerization, epoxidized soybean oil undergoes a chemical reaction to form aerylated compounds, while in Reaction with Maleic Anhydride, maleic anhydride is reacted with epoxidized soybean oil to produce aerylated products. Both methods result in the creation of aerylated epoxidized soybean oil, which finds application in various industries such as coatings, adhesives, and plastics.</p></p>
