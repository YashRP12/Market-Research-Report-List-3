<p><p>The lithium and battery materials market encompasses various types of materials used in the production of batteries. This includes anode materials, which store and release lithium ions during charging and discharging; negative electrode materials, which interact with the anode to facilitate the movement of ions; electrolytes, which conduct ions between the electrodes; and diaphragms, which prevent the mixing of materials within the battery. Each of these materials plays a crucial role in the overall performance and efficiency of lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
