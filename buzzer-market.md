<p><p>Piezo buzzers and magnetic buzzers are two common types of buzzers available in the market. Piezo buzzers use a piezoelectric crystal to produce sound when voltage is applied, creating a clear and high-pitched noise. On the other hand, magnetic buzzers use an electromagnet and a diaphragm to produce sound when an electrical signal is applied. Both types of buzzers are widely used in various applications such as alarms, timers, and communication devices due to their effectiveness and reliability.</p></p>
