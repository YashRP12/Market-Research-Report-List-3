<p><p>Window Insulation Film Market has two main types: Crosslinked Type and Non-Crosslinked Type. </p><p>Crosslinked Type films are manufactured using a method that links molecules together, increasing the strength and durability of the film. These films are ideal for long-term use and offer enhanced insulation properties. </p><p>Non-Crosslinked Type films are created without the linking process, making them more flexible and easier to install. However, they may not be as durable or effective in providing insulation compared to Crosslinked Type films. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
