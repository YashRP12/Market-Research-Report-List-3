<p><p>Fire-resisting sleeves are designed to protect cables and wires from fire and heat damage. There are three main types of fire-resisting sleeves on the market: tube cartridge type, spiral wound type, and hasp type. Tube cartridge type sleeves are easy to install and remove, providing quick protection for cables. Spiral wound type sleeves are flexible and ideal for curved or irregular surfaces. Hasp type sleeves have a locking mechanism for added security and are commonly used in industrial settings for long-term protection.</p></p>
