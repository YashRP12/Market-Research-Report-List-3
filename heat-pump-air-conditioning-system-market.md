<p><p>Heat pump air-conditioning systems come in various types, including air to water monobloc, air to water cylinder, ground/water to water, air to water split, and exhaust air market. Air to water monobloc systems consist of an outdoor unit connected to indoor heating or cooling units. Air to water cylinder systems use a hot water tank in combination with the outdoor unit. Ground/water to water systems utilize heat from the ground or water sources. Air to water split systems separate the indoor and outdoor units. Exhaust air systems utilize waste heat from ventilation systems to provide heating or cooling.</p></p>
