<p><p>Polypropylene industrial tapes are widely used in various industries for packaging, sealing, and bonding applications. The market is segmented into single-sided tape and double-sided tape. Single-sided tapes have adhesive on only one side, making them suitable for simple applications like surface protection and general sealing. Double-sided tapes have adhesive on both sides, providing stronger bonding and are used for mounting, splicing, and laminating applications. Both types of tapes offer high tensile strength, durability, and resistance to moisture and chemicals.</p></p>
