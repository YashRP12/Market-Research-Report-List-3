<p><strong>The Magnetic Plastics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structured Magnetic Plastic</li><li>Compound Magnetic Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Structured magnetic plastics are materials with a uniform arrangement of magnetic particles, allowing for precise control of magnetic properties. Compound magnetic plastics, on the other hand, contain a mixture of magnetic particles and non-magnetic materials, offering a balance of magnetic properties and mechanical strength. Structured magnetic plastics are ideal for applications where specific magnetic properties are required, such as sensors and actuators, while compound magnetic plastics are commonly used in more general applications where both magnetic and mechanical properties are important.</p></p>
