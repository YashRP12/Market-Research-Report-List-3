They are mainly used in Arctic regions for long-range missions.</p><p>LNG-powered icebreakers use liquefied natural gas as fuel, offering a cleaner and more environmentally friendly option compared to diesel-powered icebreakers. They are becoming increasingly popular as the maritime industry looks for more sustainable solutions.</p></p>
