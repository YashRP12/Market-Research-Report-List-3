<p><p>Wireless smart lighting controls systems offer various types of communication protocols such as Zigbee, WiFi, Bluetooth, Z-Wave, and EnOcean. Zigbee is widely used for its low power consumption and mesh networking capabilities. WiFi provides high-speed connectivity and seamless integration with other smart devices. Bluetooth offers short-range connectivity for easy installation and control. Z-Wave ensures reliable communication with low interference. EnOcean is known for its self-powered operation through energy harvesting. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the smart lighting controls market.</p></p>
