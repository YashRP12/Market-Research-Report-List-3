<p><strong>The Automotive Retainer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push Type</li><li>Threaded Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive retainers are fasteners used to secure various components in vehicles. There are three main types of automotive retainers: push type, threaded type, and others. Push type retainers are simply inserted into pre-drilled holes and require no additional tools for installation. Threaded type retainers, on the other hand, have screws or bolts that are twisted into place to secure components. The "others" category includes specialty retainers like snap-in, quarter-turn, and expanding rivets. Each type of retainer serves a specific purpose in automotive assembly.</p></p>
