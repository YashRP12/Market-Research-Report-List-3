<p><p>A Data Collection System (DCS) is a technology solution that captures and stores information from various sources. Two common market types for DCS are Cloud-Based and On-Premises. In a Cloud-Based market, the DCS is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage data through the internet. It offers scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness due to shared resources. On the other hand, an On-Premises market involves deploying the DCS within the organization's infrastructure. It provides greater control and security, but requires higher initial investment and maintenance costs.</p></p>
