<p><p>The cosmetics and personal care emulsifier market consists of two main types: oil-in-water (o/w) emulsifiers and water-in-oil (w/o) emulsifiers. An o/w emulsifier allows the dispersion of oil droplets within water, resulting in a creamy consistency. It is commonly used in skincare products such as lotions and creams. On the other hand, a w/o emulsifier enables the dispersion of water droplets within oil, creating a thick texture. This type of emulsifier is often found in products like cold creams and foundations.</p></p>
