<p><p>Fire retardant fabric market consists of two main types: inherent fire retardant fabric and treated fire retardant fabric. Inherent fire retardant fabric is made with fibers that have fire retardant properties built into their chemical structure, providing permanent protection against flames. On the other hand, treated fire retardant fabric is regular fabric that has been treated with chemicals to make it flame resistant. Both types of fabrics are widely used in industries where fire safety is a priority, such as in home furnishings, automotive and protective clothing.</p></p>
