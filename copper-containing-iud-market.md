<p><p>A Copper-containing IUD (Intrauterine Device) is a form of birth control that is inserted into the uterus to prevent pregnancy. Factors such as increasing awareness about contraception, rising female workforce participation, and the need for effective and long-lasting birth control methods in developing countries are driving the demand for Copper-containing IUDs.
