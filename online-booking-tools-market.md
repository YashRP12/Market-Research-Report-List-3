<p><p>The online booking tools market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based booking tools operate on remote servers, allowing users to access the software through the internet. These solutions are often provided as a service and offer flexibility, scalability, and low maintenance costs. On-premises booking tools, on the other hand, require installation and maintenance on the user's own servers. While providing control and data security, they may entail higher upfront costs, dedicated IT infrastructure, and limited remote access.</p></p>
