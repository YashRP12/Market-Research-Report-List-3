<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Planarization (CMP) consumables market consists of various types such as CMP slurries, CMP pads, CMP pad conditioners, and others. CMP slurries are abrasive liquids used to chemically remove material during the planarization process. CMP pads are the surfaces where wafer materials undergo polishing. CMP pad conditioners are used to maintain the efficacy of CMP pads. Other consumables include backing films, polishing pads, and cleaning solutions essential for the CMP process. These consumables play a crucial role in achieving high-quality planarization results in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
