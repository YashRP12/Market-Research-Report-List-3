<p><ul><li>Multi-Pocket E-Beam Guns</li><li>Single-Pocket E-Beam Guns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The E-Beam Guns market includes two major types: Multi-Pocket E-Beam Guns and Single-Pocket E-Beam Guns. Multi-Pocket E-Beam Guns consist of multiple pockets or crucibles that hold different materials. This enables the simultaneous evaporation of multiple materials during thin film coating processes. On the other hand, Single-Pocket E-Beam Guns have only one pocket and are typically used for depositing a single material at a time. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace by providing precise and efficient material deposition capabilities.</p></p>
