<p><ul><li>Brush Type</li><li>Brushless Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiator fan motors can be classified into two main types: brush type and brushless type. Brush type motors use brushes to conduct current between the stationary and rotating parts, resulting in friction and wear over time. On the other hand, brushless type motors eliminate the need for brushes, providing better efficiency, durability, and reduced noise. As a result, brushless type motors are becoming increasingly popular in the radiator fan motor market due to their superior performance and reliability.</p></p>
