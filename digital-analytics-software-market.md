<p><p>Digital analytics software can be categorized into two types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises software is installed and operated on a company's own infrastructure, allowing organizations to have full control over their data. This type of software requires hardware, maintenance, and IT support. Conversely, cloud-based software is hosted on a remote server and accessed via the internet. It eliminates the need for hardware and maintenance costs, providing flexibility and scalability. Cloud-based solutions also offer real-time data analysis, collaborative features, and easier integration with other business tools.</p></p>
