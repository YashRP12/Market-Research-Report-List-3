<p><p>Commercial baggage handling systems include RFID systems and barcode systems. Both systems help streamline baggage handling operations, improve accuracy in tracking baggage, and enhance overall efficiency in airport operations. The RFID commercial baggage handling system offers real-time tracking and monitoring capabilities, while the barcode system provides a cost-effective tracking solution for airports.</p></p>
