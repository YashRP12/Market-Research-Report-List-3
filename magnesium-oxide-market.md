<p><strong>The Magnesium Oxide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dead-Burned Magnesium Oxide (DBM)</li><li>Fused Magnesium Oxide (FM)</li><li>Caustic Calcined Magnesium Oxide (CCM)</li><li>Synthetic Magnesium Oxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnesium Oxide is used in various industries and comes in different types. Dead-Burned Magnesium Oxide (DBM) is produced by heating raw magnesite in a rotary kiln at high temperatures. Fused Magnesium Oxide (FM) is made by melting magnesite in an electric arc furnace. Caustic Calcined Magnesium Oxide (CCM) is obtained by calcining magnesite at lower temperatures. Synthetic Magnesium Oxide is produced through chemical reactions. Each type serves different purposes and has unique properties, making them suitable for various applications.</p></p>
