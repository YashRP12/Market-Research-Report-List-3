<p><p>Residential air conditioners market consists of various components such as compressors, condensers, metering devices, cooling coils, air handling units, and duct systems. The compressor is responsible for circulating refrigerant, while the condenser releases heat outside. The metering device regulates the flow of refrigerant, while the cooling coil absorbs heat from indoor air. The air handling unit distributes cool air throughout the house, and the duct system is used to channel air to different rooms. Each component plays a crucial role in the overall functioning of a residential air conditioning system.</p></p>
