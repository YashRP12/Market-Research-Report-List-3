<p><p>The combined heat and power system market consists of various types such as gas and steam turbine, reciprocating engine, fuel cell, and microturbine. Gas and steam turbine systems use a combination of gas and steam to generate power efficiently. Reciprocating engine systems use internal combustion engines to produce electricity and heat simultaneously. Fuel cell systems generate power through an electrochemical process.
