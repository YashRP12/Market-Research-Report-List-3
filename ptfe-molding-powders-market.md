<p><strong>The PTFE Molding Powders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PTFE molding powders are commonly categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I powders are highly crystalline with excellent mechanical properties and wear resistance, making them ideal for applications requiring high performance and durability. On the other hand, Type II powders are less crystalline and exhibit superior thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for applications in harsh environments with high temperatures and corrosive chemicals. Both types of PTFE molding powders offer unique properties to cater to different industry needs.</p></p>
