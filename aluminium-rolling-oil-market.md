<p><p>Aluminium Rolling Oil is used in both cold rolling and hot rolling processes in the aluminium industry. </p><p>Cold Rolling: This market type involves the process of reducing the thickness of aluminium sheets or coils at room temperature, resulting in increased strength and improved surface finish. Aluminium Rolling Oil for cold rolling is specifically designed to provide lubrication and cooling during this process.</p><p>Hot Rolling: In contrast, hot rolling involves shaping aluminium at high temperatures, making it easier to mold and reshape. Aluminium Rolling Oil for hot rolling is formulated to withstand high temperatures and provide effective lubrication for this process.</p></p>
