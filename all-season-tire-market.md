<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>All-season tires can be categorized into two main types: Bias tires and Radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with overlapping ply layers at a 30-40 degree angle to the direction of rotation, providing durability and puncture resistance. On the other hand, Radial tires have ply layers arranged at 90 degrees to the direction of rotation, offering better traction and fuel efficiency. Both types of tires are popular in the market for their versatility and performance in various weather conditions.</p></p>
