<p><p>Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) Services refer to the practice of delegating the recruitment process to external service providers. There are three main types of RPO services. On-Demand RPO is a flexible and scalable model, allowing companies to engage services when needed rather than on a continuous basis. Function-Based RPO focuses on specific parts of the recruitment process, such as candidate sourcing or screening. Full RPO offers end-to-end recruitment support, handling the entire process from job posting to onboarding. These options cater to diverse recruitment needs, providing companies with flexibility, specialization, and comprehensive solutions.</p></p>
