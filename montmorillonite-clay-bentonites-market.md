<p><p>Montmorillonite clay, also known as Bentonite, comes in three main types: Sodium Bentonite, Calcium Bentonite, and Potassium Bentonite. Sodium Bentonite is the most commonly used type and is known for its swelling properties, making it useful in sealing applications. Calcium Bentonite is known for its high absorbency and is often used in animal feed and wellness products. Potassium Bentonite is less common and is known for its detoxifying properties, often used in skincare products.Overall, Montmorillonite clay offers a wide range of benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
