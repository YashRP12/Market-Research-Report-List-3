<p><p>Tissue paper dispensers come in two main market types: manual and automatic. Manual dispensers require users to physically pull out individual sheets of tissue paper, while automatic dispensers dispense paper automatically with the wave of a hand or the press of a button. Manual dispensers are typically more cost-effective and are commonly found in residential and small business settings. On the other hand, automatic dispensers are often used in high-traffic public places such as airports, malls, and hospitals for their convenience and hygiene benefits.</p></p>
