<p><p>Presulfurizing catalyst market types include non-catalytic activity and weak catalytic activity. Weak catalytic activity, on the other hand, describes catalysts that have a limited ability to catalyze reactions compared to more active catalysts. Both types play important roles in the presulfurizing process, with non-catalytic activity providing structural support and weak catalytic activity contributing to sulfur removal efficiency.</p></p>
