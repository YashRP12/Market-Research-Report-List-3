<p><ul><li>Removers</li><li>Deposition Precursors</li><li>Etchants</li><li>Dopants</li><li>Esists</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lithographic chemicals market can be classified into different types based on their functions. Removers are used to remove unwanted materials from surfaces. Deposition precursors are chemicals that facilitate the deposition of thin films on substrates. Etchants are substances that selectively remove material to create patterns. Dopants are used to alter the electrical properties of materials. Resist chemicals are used to protect certain areas during etching or deposition processes. Other types of chemicals in this market may serve various specialized purposes in lithography.</p></p>
