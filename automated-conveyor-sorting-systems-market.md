On the other hand, Loop Sortation Systems utilize a continuous loop conveyor to transport and sort items efficiently. Both types of systems are integral in improving productivity, reducing labor costs, and enhancing overall throughput in warehouses and distribution centers. These systems play a vital role in optimizing logistics processes and ensuring timely delivery of goods.</p></p>
