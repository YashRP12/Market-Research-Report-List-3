<p><p>Automatic Colon Hydrotherapy Machines can be categorized into two main types: Open System and Closed System. Open System machines use a gravity-fed system where water flows in and out of the colon, while Closed System machines involve a pressurized system where water is filtered and regulated before entering the colon. Each type has its own set of advantages and drawbacks, with Open System machines being more user-friendly and cost-effective, while Closed System machines are often preferred for their controlled and hygienic process.</p></p>
