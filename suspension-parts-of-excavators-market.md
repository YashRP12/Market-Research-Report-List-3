<p><p>In the Excavators market, Suspension Parts come in various types including Rigid Connected Type, Semi-Floating, Full Floating, and Independent Mounted. Rigid Connected Type suspension connects the wheels or tracks directly to the axle, providing minimal movement. Semi-Floating suspension allows for some independent movement of the wheels or tracks in relation to the axle. Full Floating suspension provides complete independence of the wheels or tracks from the axle. Independent Mounted suspension allows each wheel or track to move independently, providing superior stability and traction in various terrain conditions.</p></p>
