<p><strong>The Concrete Repair Mortars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer-modified Cementitious Mortar</li><li>Epoxy-based Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete repair mortars are classified into two main types: polymer-modified cementitious mortar and epoxy-based mortar. Polymer-modified cementitious mortars are composed of Portland cement, polymers, and aggregates, providing good adhesion and durability. Epoxy-based mortars, on the other hand, are made of epoxy resins and hardeners, offering high strength and chemical resistance. Polymer-modified cementitious mortars are commonly used for repairing concrete structures, while epoxy-based mortars are preferred for more demanding applications where strength and durability are crucial.</p></p>
