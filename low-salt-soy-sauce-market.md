<p><ul><li>Brewed</li><li>Blended</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low salt soy sauce is available in two main market types: brewed and blended. Brewed soy sauce is made through a traditional fermentation process that involves naturally fermenting soybeans, wheat, water, and salt over a prolonged period of time. This results in a rich and complex flavor profile. On the other hand, blended soy sauce is produced by mixing brewed soy sauce with other ingredients such as water, sugar, and artificial additives. This type tends to have a milder taste and is often more affordable.</p></p>
