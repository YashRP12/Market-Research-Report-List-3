<p><p>Mobiltech textiles are used in various markets such as woven fabric, non-woven fabric, knitted fabric, and others. Woven fabric is made by interlacing two sets of yarns at right angles, providing strength and durability. Non-woven fabric is created by bonding or interlocking fibers without weaving, offering cost-effectiveness and versatility. Knitted fabric is made by interlocking yarns in a series of connected loops, providing stretch and flexibility. Other market types include specialized textiles for unique applications in the mobiltech industry.</p></p>
