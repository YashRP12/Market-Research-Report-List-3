<p><p>Centrifugation and membrane filtration are two types of mobile platelet collection systems used in the healthcare industry. Centrifugation involves the separation of platelets from other blood components through high-speed spinning, while membrane filtration utilizes porous membranes to selectively filter platelets from the blood. Both methods are designed to collect platelets efficiently and safely, with centrifugation being more commonly used due to its established effectiveness. These systems play a crucial role in providing a reliable supply of platelets for transfusion purposes, particularly in remote or mobile healthcare settings.</p></p>
