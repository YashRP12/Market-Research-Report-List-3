<p><p>OLED displays have various market types including Transparent OLEDs, Stacked OLEDs, and Inverted OLEDs. Transparent OLEDs allow for see-through displays, ideal for applications such as automotive windows or digital signage. Stacked OLEDs offer improved efficiency and brightness by layering OLED panels on top of each other. Inverted OLEDs have the cathode layer on top of the stack, allowing for more flexibility in design and potentially better performance. Each market type offers unique advantages and applications in the OLED display industry.</p></p>
