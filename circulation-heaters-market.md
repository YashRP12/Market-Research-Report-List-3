<p><p>Circulation heaters are electric devices used for heating liquids and gases in various industrial processes. They consist of a body or vessel, which houses heating elements, an insulation layer, and a sheath material that encloses the entire unit. The liquid or gas to be heated circulates through the heater, allowing efficient and precise control of temperature.</p><p>The circulation heaters market is witnessing steady growth due to the increasing demand for effective heating solutions in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, petrochemical, and food processing. These heaters offer advantages such as high heating efficiency, ease of installation, and low maintenance requirements, driving their adoption across different applications.</p><p>One of the key trends shaping the circulation heaters market is the rising demand for customized and energy-efficient heating solutions.
