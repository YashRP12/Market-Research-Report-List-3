<p><strong>The Absolute Shaft Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Turn</li><li>Single-Turn</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Absolute shaft encoders are classified into two main types - Multi-Turn and Single-Turn. Multi-Turn encoders are able to measure both the absolute position and the number of complete shaft rotations, making them suitable for applications requiring high precision over multiple revolutions. Single-Turn encoders, on the other hand, provide absolute position information for a single revolution of the shaft. Each type of encoder serves a specific purpose depending on the application's requirements for accuracy and range of motion.</p></p>
