<p><p>Waterproof surgical tapes are available in two main types: PE and non-woven fabric. PE tapes are made of a thin plastic material that is waterproof and flexible, making them ideal for use in wet environments or where flexibility is important. Non-woven fabric tapes are softer and more breathable, making them suitable for sensitive skin or longer wear times. Both types of tapes provide secure adhesion and protection for wounds during surgical procedures, catering to different needs and preferences in the medical industry.</p></p>
