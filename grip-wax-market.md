Hard wax comes in a solid form and is rubbed onto the ski base. Klister wax is sticky and is used in wet and icy conditions. Spray wax is applied as a liquid and dries onto the ski base. Each type of grip wax is designed for specific snow conditions, providing skiers with the optimal grip and glide.</p></p>
