<p><strong>The Flex Foam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Closed cell Flex Foam has a compact structure, preventing air and moisture from passing through. It is ideal for applications requiring high resistance to compression and water absorption, such as insulation and packaging. On the other hand, Open cell Flex Foam has interconnected air pockets, providing a softer and more breathable material. It is commonly used in cushioning and upholstery applications where air flow and flexibility are important. Both types cater to different market needs based on their unique properties and characteristics.</p></p>
