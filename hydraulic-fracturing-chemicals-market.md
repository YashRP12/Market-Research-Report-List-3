<p><p>Hydraulic fracturing chemicals market can be classified into various types such as Methanol, Ethylene Glycol, Diesel, Naphthalene, Ethylbenzene, Formaldehyde, and Others. Methanol is used as a base fluid in hydraulic fracturing fluids. Ethylene Glycol is used as a friction reducer. Diesel is used as a gelling agent. Naphthalene is used for controlling microbial growth. Ethylbenzene is used for enhancing oil recovery. Formaldehyde is used as a crosslinker. Other chemicals include corrosion inhibitors, surfactants, and biocides used in hydraulic fracturing operations.</p></p>
