<p><p>Structured packing is a type of structured media used in separation processes, such as distillation and absorption, to enhance efficiency and performance. It consists of stacked corrugated sheets or grids arranged in a specific pattern to create a large surface area for mass transfer. This design allows for improved contact between the liquid and vapor phases, leading to better separation of components.</p><p>The Structured Packing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.20% during the forecast period. The market growth is driven by increasing demand for structured packing in various industries, including petrochemical, chemical, and pharmaceutical. The rising need for efficient separation processes and the growing focus on sustainability and environmental regulations are also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the structured packing market is the development of advanced materials and designs to further enhance performance and efficiency. Manufacturers are investing in research and development to create innovative structured packing solutions that offer higher capacity, lower pressure drop, and reduced energy consumption.
