Lead-acid batteries are known for their low cost and high durability, although they are heavier and have a shorter lifespan compared to Li-ion batteries. Li-ion batteries are popular for their high energy density, lightweight design, and longer lifespan. NiMH batteries are a middle-ground option, offering a good balance of cost, energy density, and lifespan.
