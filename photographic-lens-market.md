<p><p>The photographic lens market can be broadly categorized into two main types: Prime Lenses and Zoom Lenses. On the other hand, zoom lenses offer variable focal lengths, allowing photographers to zoom in and out without changing the lens. They are versatile and convenient for capturing a wide range of subjects, from landscapes to wildlife. Both types cater to different photography needs and preferences.</p></p>
