<p><p>Glazed curtain wall systems are designed to provide a sleek and modern aesthetic for buildings while maximizing natural light. The different types of glazed curtain wall systems include Bright Box, Hidden Frame, Horizontal Frame, and Horizontal Box. Bright Box systems have a visually striking appearance with minimal framing visible on the exterior. Hidden Frame systems feature concealed framework for a seamless look. Horizontal Frame systems have visible horizontal mullions that divide the glass panels. Horizontal Box systems combine horizontal and vertical mullions for a structured and uniform appearance.</p></p>
