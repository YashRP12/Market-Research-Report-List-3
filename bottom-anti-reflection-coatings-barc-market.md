<p><p>Bottom Anti-Reflection Coatings (BARC) are used in the semiconductor industry to reduce reflection and improve light transmission during lithography processes. BARCs come in two main types: organic and inorganic. Organic BARCs are made of carbon-based polymers and are popular for their low cost and ease of application. Inorganic BARCs are made of materials like silicon dioxide and titanium dioxide, offering enhanced durability and resistance to etching chemicals. Both types play a crucial role in improving the accuracy and efficiency of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
