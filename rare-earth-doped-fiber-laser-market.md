<p><p>Rare Earth Doped Fiber Laser market is divided into two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser and Pulsed Fiber Laser. Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Lasers emit a continuous beam of laser light, enabling steady and uninterrupted performance suitable for various applications such as material processing and medical procedures. On the other hand, Pulsed Fiber Lasers emit short bursts of high-intensity laser pulses, making them ideal for applications requiring precise control and high peak powers, such as marking, cutting, and welding.</p></p>
