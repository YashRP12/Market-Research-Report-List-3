<p><p>Explosion-proof fans are specialized ventilation systems designed to prevent the ignition of flammable gases, vapors, dust, or fibers in hazardous environments. The two main types of explosion-proof fans are centrifugal fans and axial flow fans. Explosion-proof centrifugal fans use rotating impellers to increase airflow, while explosion-proof axial flow fans use propellers to move air in a straight line. Both types of fans are crucial in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and mining where the risk of explosion is high.</p></p>
