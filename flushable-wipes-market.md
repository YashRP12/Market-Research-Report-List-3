<p><p>Flushable wipes market can be divided into three main types based on the materials used in their production: biodegradable fibers, non-biodegradable fibers, and latex binders. Biodegradable fibers are eco-friendly and break down easily in water, reducing environmental impact. Non-biodegradable fibers are more durable but may cause clogging in sewage systems. Latex binders are used to hold the wipes together but may not break down easily in water. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of sustainability and functionality.</p></p>
