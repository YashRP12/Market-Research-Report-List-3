<p><p>Radial tires are commonly divided into three main types based on their construction materials: all-steel radial tires, semi-steel radial tires, and full-fiber radial tires. All-steel radial tires feature steel belts and are known for their durability and strength, making them suitable for heavy-duty vehicles. Semi-steel radial tires have a mix of steel and other materials, offering a balance between performance and cost. Full-fiber radial tires use fibers like polyester or rayon for reinforcement, providing good traction and a comfortable ride.</p></p>
