<p><p>Laboratory proficiency testing is a quality assurance process used in laboratories to assess and validate their testing capabilities and ensure accurate and reliable results. The aim is to identify any potential deficiencies or areas for improvement and ensure the overall quality and competence of testing laboratories.</p><p>The laboratory proficiency testing market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for quality testing services across various sectors, including pharmaceuticals, healthcare, food and beverages, environment, and others.
