<p><p>Silicone molding compounds are used in various types of molding processes such as press molding and injection molding. Press molding involves applying pressure to the compound in a mold to shape it into the desired form. On the other hand, injection molding involves injecting the compound into a mold cavity under high pressure to create the final product. Both types of molding processes are widely used in industries such as automotive, electronics, and medical devices for producing high-quality silicone components.</p></p>
