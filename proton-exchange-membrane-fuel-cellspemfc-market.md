In Compressed Gaseous Hydrogen PEMFC, hydrogen is stored under high pressure in tanks. Cryogenic Liquid Hydrogen PEMFC utilizes liquid hydrogen stored at extremely low temperatures. Hydrides market PEMFC involves the use of solid-state metal hydrides to store and release hydrogen. Each type of PEMFC has its own advantages and challenges in terms of efficiency, cost, and practicality.</p></p>
