<p><p>The Carbon Monoxide Monitor market can be divided into two main types: fixed monitors and portable monitors. Fixed monitors are installed in a specific location to constantly monitor levels of carbon monoxide in the air. They are commonly used in industrial settings or in homes with gas appliances. Portable monitors, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be carried around to different locations to spot-check for carbon monoxide levels. They are often used by firefighters, emergency responders, or individuals concerned about indoor air quality.</p></p>
