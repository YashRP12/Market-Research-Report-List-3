<p><p>Elastomers for Pharmaceuticals refer to materials that have elastic properties and are used in pharmaceutical applications. The two main types are Thermoset Elastomers and Thermoplastic Elastomers. Thermoset Elastomers are cross-linked polymers that offer high durability and chemical resistance. On the other hand, Thermoplastic Elastomers are linear polymers that can be melted and reshaped, offering flexibility and easy processing. Both types of elastomers play a crucial role in pharmaceutical packaging, drug delivery systems, and medical devices.</p></p>
