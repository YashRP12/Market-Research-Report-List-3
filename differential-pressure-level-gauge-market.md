<p><p>A Differential-Pressure Level Gauge is a type of level measurement device that operates based on the principle of measuring the difference in pressure between two points in a vessel or a tank. This gauge can be classified into two main market types: Mechanical Level Gauge and Electronic Level Gauge. Mechanical Level Gauge uses mechanical components, such as floats or displacers, to measure the liquid level. On the other hand, Electronic Level Gauge relies on electronic sensors and transmitters to provide accurate and real-time level measurements. Both types serve the purpose of gauging the level of liquids in industrial applications.</p></p>
