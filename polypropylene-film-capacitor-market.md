<p><p>Polypropylene film capacitors are classified into two main types: Plain Polypropylene Film Capacitors and Metallised Polypropylene Film Capacitors. Plain Polypropylene Film Capacitors are made of a single layer of polypropylene film which provides high insulation resistance and stability, making them suitable for high voltage applications. On the other hand, Metallised Polypropylene Film Capacitors have a thin metal layer deposited on the film, improving their conductivity and allowing for higher capacitance values. Both types are commonly used in various electronic and electrical circuits for their reliability and performance.</p></p>
