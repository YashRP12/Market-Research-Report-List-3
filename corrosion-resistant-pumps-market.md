<p><p>The corrosion resistant pumps market includes two primary types: single impeller pumps and dual impeller pumps. Single impeller pumps are designed with only one impeller, which is a rotating component that moves fluid within the pump. These pumps are suitable for applications that require low to medium flow rates. On the other hand, dual impeller pumps have two impellers, offering higher flow rates and greater pumping efficiency. These pumps are commonly used in industrial settings where higher fluid volumes need to be moved. Both types of pumps are corrosion resistant, ensuring durability and longevity in corrosive environments.</p></p>
