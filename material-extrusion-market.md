<p><p>Material extrusion market can be categorized into three main types: metal, plastic, and others. Metal extrusion involves pushing a metal material through a die to create a desired shape or product. Plastic extrusion is similar, but uses plastic materials instead. The "others" category includes various materials such as ceramics, food products, and biomaterials. Each type of extrusion market serves different industries and applications, with metal extrusion commonly used in automotive and aerospace sectors, plastic extrusion in packaging and construction, and others in specialized fields like medical devices.</p></p>
