<p><p>Hydrogenation petroleum resin market includes three main types: hydrogenated C9 petroleum resin, hydrogenated C5 petroleum resin, and hydrogenated DCPD petroleum resin. Hydrogenated C9 petroleum resin is known for its excellent heat resistance and adhesion properties, making it ideal for hot melt adhesives and coatings. Hydrogenated C5 petroleum resin offers good compatibility with various polymers and is commonly used in adhesives, sealants, and coatings. Hydrogenated DCPD petroleum resin is favored for its low color and good thermal stability, making it suitable for printing inks and rubber compounding.</p></p>
