<p><p>Drive-by-wire technology refers to electronic control systems that replace traditional mechanical connections in vehicles. Acceleration by wire enables control of vehicle speed through electronic throttle control. Brake by wire replaces conventional brake pedal with an electronic system. Steering by wire uses electronic signals for steering control instead of a mechanical steering column. Shift by wire replaces manual gear shifting with electronic transmission controls. Park by wire allows electronic activation of parking brakes. These technologies enhance vehicle performance, responsiveness, and safety by eliminating mechanical limitations and providing precise control.</p></p>
