<p><p>Linear Queue Management System is segmented into different market types based on the structure and functionality of the queue system. Structured Queue uses a systematic approach with designated waiting lines and service points. Unstructured Queue allows customers to form lines informally. Kiosk-Based Queue enables customers to check in and receive a queuing number electronically. Moving Queue uses mobile apps or devices to notify customers about their turn in the queue. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences of businesses and customers.</p></p>
