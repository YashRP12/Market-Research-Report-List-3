<p><strong>The Intelligent Sorting System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Sortation Systems</li><li>Loop Sortation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intelligent sorting systems are used in industries for efficient and accurate sorting of items. Linear sortation systems involve a straight line of conveyors with items sorted at various points along the line. Loop sortation systems, on the other hand, use a continuous loop of conveyors with items sorted as they circulate around the loop. Both systems offer high-speed sorting capabilities, increased productivity, and reduced errors. Linear sortation systems are ideal for high-throughput operations, while loop sortation systems are suitable for smaller facilities with limited space.</p></p>
