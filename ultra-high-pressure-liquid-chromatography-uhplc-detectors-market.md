<p><p>Ultra High Pressure Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC) detectors are vital components in analytical instruments used for separating and identifying compounds in a sample. The main types of UHPLC detectors include UV-Visible detectors, which measure the absorbance of light by the sample; Refractive Index detectors, which detect changes in refractive index; Evaporative Light Scattering Detectors (ELSD), which measure light scattered by the sample; Fluorescence detectors, which measure fluorescence emitted by the sample; and other detectors like conductivity detectors, mass spectrometers, and electrochemical detectors. Each type of detector has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different applications in analytical chemistry.</p></p>
