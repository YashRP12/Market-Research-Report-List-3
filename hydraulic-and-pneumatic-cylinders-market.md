<p><p>Hydraulic and pneumatic cylinders are utilized in various industries for different applications. Single action cylinders operate in one direction, providing force in one direction only. Double rod cylinders have rods extending from both ends, allowing for force application in both directions. Telescopic cylinders consist of multiple nested sections that extend and retract, providing a longer stroke length. Each type of cylinder offers unique advantages for specific applications, such as precision control for single action cylinders, versatility for double rod cylinders, and extended stroke capabilities for telescopic cylinders.</p></p>
