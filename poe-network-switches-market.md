<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) network switches are devices that combine both networking and power delivery capabilities, allowing for the transmission of data and power over a single Ethernet cable. This technology is commonly used in applications where it may be difficult to provide separate power supplies, such as in IP cameras, wireless access points, and VoIP phones.</p><p>The PoE network switches market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing adoption of IP-based devices in various industries. The ability to simplify installation and reduce costs by eliminating the need for separate power sources is a key factor contributing to the market growth. Additionally, the growing demand for high-speed connectivity and the increasing trend towards smart homes and smart buildings are further fueling the adoption of PoE network switches.</p><p>The market is also witnessing the emergence of new trends, such as the integration of advanced features like network management capabilities, high power delivery, and support for emerging technologies like IoT devices.
