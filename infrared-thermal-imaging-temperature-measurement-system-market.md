Temperature Measuring Cameras are handheld devices that use infrared technology to accurately measure the temperature of an object or environment. Temperature Measuring Blackbodies, on the other hand, are stationary devices that emit a known temperature and are used to calibrate and validate the accuracy of temperature measuring cameras.
