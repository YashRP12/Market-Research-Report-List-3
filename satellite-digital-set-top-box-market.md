There are different market types for these set-top boxes, including OTT (Over the Top) and DTT (Direct Terrestrial Transmission). OTT refers to content delivered over the internet, bypassing traditional cable or satellite providers. DTT, on the other hand, relies on terrestrial transmitters to deliver digital signals directly to households. Both market types offer consumers more options in accessing digital content and television channels.</p></p>
