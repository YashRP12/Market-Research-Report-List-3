<p><p>The liquid macrofiltration filters market consists of several types of filters, each serving different purposes. The granular micro filter employs a bed of granular media to filter out particles and impurities from liquids. Leaf tubular and press filters use a series of conically shaped leaf and tubular elements to effectively filter liquids. Bag filters are made of porous bags that capture solid particles. Belt filter presses use a continuous, moving belt to remove particulate matter from liquids. Other filters in the market include cartridge filters, disc filters, and sand filters, among others.</p></p>
