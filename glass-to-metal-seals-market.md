<p><strong>The Glass-to-metal Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Matched Seals</li><li>Compression Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass-to-metal seals are widely used in various industrial applications due to their ability to create a hermetic and durable connection between glass and metal components. Matched seals are specifically designed to have similar coefficients of thermal expansion, ensuring compatibility between the glass and metal materials used. This type of seal is commonly used in applications where temperature changes occur frequently. On the other hand, compression seals involve applying pressure to compress the glass and metal components together, creating a tight seal. These seals are used in applications where high pressure or extreme conditions are involved.</p></p>
