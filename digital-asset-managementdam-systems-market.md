AI-powered features such as automatic tagging, metadata extraction, and intelligent search capabilities are simplifying asset management tasks and improving efficiency.</p><p>Cloud-based DAM systems have also gained popularity due to their scalability, accessibility, and cost-effectiveness. These systems allow users to store and access assets from anywhere, facilitating remote work and global collaboration.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing demand for personalized customer experiences and consistent branding across various channels has increased the need for DAM systems.
