These systems enable companies to track and trace forklifts, monitor driver performance, and remotely diagnose and address maintenance issues, leading to improved overall operational efficiency.</p><p>Furthermore, the integration of advanced technologies, such as IoT and telematics, into forklift fleet management systems is presenting new growth opportunities.
