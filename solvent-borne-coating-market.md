<p><p>Solvent-Borne Coating market types include hydrocarbon solvent, oxygenated solvent, and others. Hydrocarbon solvents are derived from petroleum and include mineral spirits, naphtha, and turpentine. Oxygenated solvents are produced through chemical reactions involving oxygen and include alcohols, esters, and ketones. Other types of solvents may include glycol ethers and aromatic solvents. Each type of solvent has unique properties that affect the coating's performance, drying time, and environmental impact. Customers choose the solvent type based on specific application requirements and regulations.</p></p>
