<p><p>Resin latex market is segmented into two main types: Natural Rubber Latex and Synthetic Rubber Latex. Natural Rubber Latex is obtained from the sap of rubber trees and is known for its excellent elasticity and resistance to tear. Synthetic Rubber Latex, on the other hand, is a man-made alternative that offers similar properties to natural rubber but is produced through chemical processes. Both types of latex are widely used in various industries for manufacturing products such as adhesives, coatings, and sealants.</p></p>
