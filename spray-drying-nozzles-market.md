<p><p>Spray drying nozzles market types include Swirlchamber Design and Slotted Core Design. Swirlchamber Design nozzles are characterized by a swirling flow pattern that helps in achieving fine droplet size distribution. On the other hand, Slotted Core Design nozzles have a unique design with a slotted core that allows for precise control over droplet size and spray pattern. Both types of nozzles are commonly used in spray drying processes for industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
