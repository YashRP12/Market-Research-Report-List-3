<p><strong>The Altimeter System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Barometric Leveling</li><li>Laser Altimetry</li><li>Radar Altimetry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Altimeter System market consists of three main types: Barometric Leveling, Laser Altimetry, and Radar Altimetry. Barometric Leveling uses atmospheric pressure to determine altitude, while Laser Altimetry utilizes lasers to measure heights above the Earth's surface. Radar Altimetry uses radio waves to calculate altitude by measuring the time it takes for the signal to bounce back from the ground. Each type has specific advantages and applications in industries such as aviation, meteorology, and geology.</p></p>
