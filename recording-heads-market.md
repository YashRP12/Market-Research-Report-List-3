<p><ul><li>Magnetoresistive Head</li><li>Giant Magnetoresistive Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Recording heads are crucial components in magnetic storage devices such as hard drives. Two common types in the market are Magnetoresistive Heads (MR heads) and Giant Magnetoresistive Heads (GMR heads). MR heads use the effect of resistance changes in a magnetic field to write data, while GMR heads utilize a stack of magnetic materials to enhance sensitivity and efficiency. GMR heads are newer and provide higher storage densities and faster read/write speeds, making them popular in modern storage technologies.</p></p>
