<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Patch Cord Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic patch cords are used for transmitting data in fiber optic networks. There are two main types of fiber optic patch cords: single-mode and multimode. </p><p>Single-mode patch cords are designed for long-distance communication and have a smaller core size which allows light to travel in a straight line, resulting in higher bandwidth and greater transmission distances. </p><p>Multimode patch cords, on the other hand, are better suited for shorter distances and have a larger core size that allows multiple modes of light to pass through, limiting bandwidth and transmission distances.</p></p>
