<p><p>Mechanical Ship Loading System refers to the use of mechanical equipment such as conveyors, cranes, and grabs to load bulk materials onto ships efficiently and quickly. On the other hand, Pneumatic Ship Loading System utilizes compressed air to transport materials from storage facilities onto ships. Companies can choose between these two types of systems based on their specific needs and requirements for their ship loading operations.</p></p>
