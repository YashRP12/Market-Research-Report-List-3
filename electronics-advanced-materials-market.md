<p><p>Electronics advanced materials market focuses on technologies like Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) for depositing thin layers of materials onto electronic devices. CVD involves the chemical reaction of gases to form a solid material on a substrate, while ALD involves the sequential exposure of reactant gases to the substrate surface. These processes are crucial for creating advanced materials with specific properties, such as high conductivity, corrosion resistance, and improved performance in electronic applications.</p></p>
