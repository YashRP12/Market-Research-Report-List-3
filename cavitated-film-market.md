<p><p>Cavitated film is a type of packaging material characterized by tiny voids or cavities that are intentionally formed during the film extrusion process. Additionally, the rising awareness about sustainable packaging materials is further fueling the market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the cavitated film market include the development of bio-based cavitated films, which are made from renewable sources such as corn starch or sugarcane.
