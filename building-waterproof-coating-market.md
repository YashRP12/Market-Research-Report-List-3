<p><ul><li>Dry</li><li>Liquid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Building waterproof coating market can be divided into two main types: Dry and Liquid coatings. Dry coatings come in powder form and are typically mixed with water or a solvent on-site before application. They are easy to store and transport but can be more labor-intensive to apply. Liquid coatings, on the other hand, are pre-mixed and ready to use. They are generally easier and faster to apply but may have a shorter shelf life. Both types offer effective protection against water damage for buildings.</p></p>
