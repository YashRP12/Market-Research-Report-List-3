<p><ul><li>Towbarless</li><li>With Towbar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light Aircraft Tractors come in two main types: towbarless and with towbar. Towbarless tractors are designed to lift the aircraft without the need for a towbar, offering more maneuverability and efficiency. On the other hand, tractors with towbar require the use of a towbar to move the aircraft, making them less flexible but more cost-effective. Both types cater to different needs in the market, with towbarless tractors being popular for their ease of use and faster operations, while tractors with towbar are preferred for their simplicity and affordability.</p></p>
