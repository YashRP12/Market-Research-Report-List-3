<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cleanroom monitoring systems can be categorized into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic systems utilize sensors and software to continuously monitor parameters like temperature, humidity, particle levels, and airflow in real-time. These systems require minimal human intervention and can provide instant alerts in case of any deviation from set parameters. On the other hand, semi-automatic systems involve manual monitoring and data collection by personnel, with some automation in place for data analysis and reporting. Both types of systems are crucial for ensuring the cleanliness and efficiency of cleanroom environments.</p></p>
