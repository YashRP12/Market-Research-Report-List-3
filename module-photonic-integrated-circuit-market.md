<p><p>The Module Photonic Integrated Circuit market consists of two main types: Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit (PIC) and Active Photonic Integrated Circuit. Passive PICs mainly control and manipulate light signals without requiring an external power source, making them suitable for applications like signal filtering and routing. On the other hand, Active PICs incorporate active components like laser diodes to amplify or modulate light signals, enabling functionalities such as signal amplification and data transmission. These two types cater to different needs in the rapidly growing photonic integrated circuit market.</p></p>
