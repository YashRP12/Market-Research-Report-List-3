<p><strong>The Flexible Acrylic Emulsion Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible acrylic emulsion market is divided into two main types - Type I and Type II. Type I emulsions are water-based and are used for interior applications such as painting walls, ceilings, and other surfaces. These emulsions offer high durability, flexibility, and resistance to weathering. On the other hand, Type II emulsions are solvent-based and are typically used for exterior applications such as painting concrete, driveways, and other outdoor surfaces. Both types of emulsions provide excellent adhesion and durability for different types of surfaces.</p></p>
