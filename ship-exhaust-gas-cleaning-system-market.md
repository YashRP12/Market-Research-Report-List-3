<p><p>Ship exhaust gas cleaning systems refer to technologies used to reduce pollutants emitted from ship engines, primarily sulfur oxides. The market includes three main types: Open Loop Scrubbers, which use seawater to wash exhaust gases; Closed Loop Scrubbers, which use a circulation system for scrubbing; and Hybrid Scrubbers, which can operate in both open and closed loop modes. Each type offers different benefits and considerations for ship operators in terms of cost, efficiency, and environmental impact.</p></p>
