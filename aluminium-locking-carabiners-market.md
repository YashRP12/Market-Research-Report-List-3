<p><p>Aluminium locking carabiners come in three different types: non-locking, manual locking, and auto-locking. Non-locking carabiners are the most basic type and are not secured closed, while manual locking carabiners require the user to twist or screw a sleeve to secure them closed. Auto-locking carabiners feature a spring-loaded mechanism that automatically locks the gate closed when released. Each type offers varying levels of security and ease of use, catering to different preferences and use cases in the market.</p></p>
