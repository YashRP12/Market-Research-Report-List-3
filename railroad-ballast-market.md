<p><p>Railroad ballast market types include granite, trap rock, quartzite, dolomite, and others. Granite ballast is known for its durability and resistance to weathering. Trap rock is a dense rock used for high-traffic railways. Quartzite ballast is highly resistant to abrasion. Dolomite ballast is known for its hardness and durability. Other types of ballast may include limestone, basalt, and sandstone, each with unique properties suitable for different railway applications.</p></p>
