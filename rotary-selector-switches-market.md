<p><p>Rotary selector switches come in two main types: single-pole and multi-pole. Single-pole rotary selector switches have only one input and can be used to select one of multiple options by rotating the switch. Multi-pole rotary selector switches, on the other hand, have multiple inputs and allow for the selection of more complex configurations or options. Both types of switches are commonly used in various industries for controlling different functions or processes by manually selecting the desired setting.</p></p>
