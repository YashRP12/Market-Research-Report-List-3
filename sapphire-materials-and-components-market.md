<p><p>Sapphire materials and components markets can be classified into two main types: skarn type and hydrothermal type. Skarn type sapphire is usually found in metamorphic rocks and is characterized by its high hardness and resistance to wear and corrosion. On the other hand, hydrothermal type sapphire is formed through hydrothermal processes and is known for its high clarity and color consistency. Both types of sapphire are widely used in various industries such as electronics, optics, and aerospace due to their exceptional properties.</p></p>
