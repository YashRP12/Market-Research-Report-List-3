<p><strong>The Auto-transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Phase</li><li>Three-Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Auto-transformers are categorized into two types based on their phase configuration: Single Phase and Three-Phase. Single Phase auto-transformers are designed for applications that require power transfer between two circuits of the same voltage but different current levels. On the other hand, Three-Phase auto-transformers are used in systems where power needs to be transferred between three circuits with different levels of current. Both types of auto-transformers offer benefits such as compact size, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness compared to traditional transformers.</p></p>
