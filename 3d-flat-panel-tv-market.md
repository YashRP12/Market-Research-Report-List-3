<p><p>Polarized 3D flat panel TVs use polarizing lenses to display separate images for each eye, creating a 3D effect. Shutter 3D TVs alternate between displaying images for the left and right eye, synced with shutter glasses worn by the viewer. Other types of 3D flat panel TVs may use different technologies such as autostereoscopic displays or lenticular lenses to achieve a 3D effect without the need for special glasses. These different market types cater to various preferences and technological advancements in the 3D TV industry.</p></p>
