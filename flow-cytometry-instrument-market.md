<p><strong>The Flow Cytometry Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell Analyzers</li><li>Cell Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flow cytometry instruments can be divided into two main types: cell analyzers and cell sorters. Cell analyzers are designed to analyze and quantify different properties of cells, such as size, complexity, and presence of specific proteins. On the other hand, cell sorters have the additional capability of physically separating different types of cells based on these properties. Both types of instruments are crucial tools in research, diagnostics, and drug development in fields such as immunology, oncology, and stem cell research.</p></p>
