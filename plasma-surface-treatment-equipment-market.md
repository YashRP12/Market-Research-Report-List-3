<p><p>Plasma surface treatment equipment can be categorized into two main types: low pressure/vacuum plasma surface treatment equipment and atmospheric plasma surface treatment equipment. Low pressure/vacuum plasma treatment equipment operates in a controlled environment with reduced pressure, while atmospheric plasma treatment equipment operates at ambient pressure. Low pressure/vacuum plasma treatment equipment is commonly used for precision applications, while atmospheric plasma treatment equipment is often used for larger scale and cost-effective treatments. Both types of equipment play a significant role in enhancing surface properties and promoting adhesion in various industries.</p></p>
