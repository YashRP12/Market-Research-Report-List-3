<p><strong>The PU Foam Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PU foam sealants are commonly categorized into one-component and two-component markets based on their formulation and application process. One-component sealants come ready-to-use and cure on contact with moisture in the environment, making them easy to apply. On the other hand, two-component sealants require mixing of two separate components before application, allowing for better control over curing time and flexibility in the sealing process. Both types of sealants have their own advantages and are widely used in construction, automotive, and other industries.</p></p>
