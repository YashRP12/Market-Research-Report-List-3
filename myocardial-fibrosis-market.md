<p><p>Myocardial fibrosis is a condition characterized by the excessive buildup of fibrous tissue in the heart muscle (myocardium), leading to rigidity and thickening of the heart wall. This condition is usually a result of various heart diseases, such as myocardial infarction, cardiomyopathy, hypertension, and heart failure. The fibrous tissue formation impairs the heart's ability to contract and relax properly, leading to reduced cardiac function and potentially lethal complications.</p><p>The myocardial fibrosis market is projected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period.
