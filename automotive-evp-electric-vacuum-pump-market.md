<p><p>The Automotive EVP (Electric Vacuum Pump) market includes three main types: Diaphragm Type, Leaf Type, and Swing Piston Type. Diaphragm Type EVPs use a flexible diaphragm to create vacuum pressure, while Leaf Type EVPs use a rotating leaf design. Swing Piston Type EVPs utilize a swinging motion to generate vacuum pressure. Each type of EVP offers unique benefits and features, catering to different automotive applications and requirements in terms of efficiency, performance, and durability.</p></p>
