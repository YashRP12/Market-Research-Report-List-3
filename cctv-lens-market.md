They are often used in small spaces or for monitoring specific areas. On the other hand, zoom lenses have variable focal lengths, allowing users to adjust the magnification levels. Zoom lenses are versatile and useful for observing distant or changing subjects. Both fixed focus and zoom lenses cater to different surveillance needs in the CCTV industry.</p></p>
