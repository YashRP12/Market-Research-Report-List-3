<p><ul><li>Hollow Fabric</li><li>Solid Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Three-dimensional woven fabric market can be classified into two main types, including hollow fabric and solid fabric. Hollow fabric consists of interconnected tubes or cavities within the fabric structure, providing lightweight and breathable properties. On the other hand, solid fabric is made up of solid yarns woven together without any hollow spaces, offering higher strength and durability. Both types cater to different consumer needs and applications, with hollow fabric being more suitable for lightweight and breathable products, while solid fabric is ideal for heavy-duty and durable products.</p></p>
