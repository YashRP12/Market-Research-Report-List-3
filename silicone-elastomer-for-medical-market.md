<p><p>Silicone elastomers for medical applications are available in two main market types: Room Temperature Vulcanizing (RTV) and High Temperature Vulcanizing (HTV). RTV silicone elastomers are typically used for applications that require quick curing at room temperature, making them ideal for prototyping and smaller scale production. On the other hand, HTV silicone elastomers require high temperatures for curing, leading to higher physical properties and resistance to heat. HTV silicones are commonly used in medical devices and components that require superior durability and performance.</p></p>
